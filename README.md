15:00 - 16:00 [Summer Music in the Courtyard: Brass Over Bridges](https://sccl.bibliocommons.com/events/69e14e38bd9a3c316a5364de)

Based in San Francisco, Brass Over Bridges is an ensemble built on the belief that music is all about connection. Inspired by the cultural diversity of the Bay Area, the quintet seeks to engage audiences by bridging barriers of style and artistic discipline. The group cares deeply about expanding the standard brass repertoire to be more representative of the modern cultural landscape, frequently commissioning new works by diverse composers such as Julie Barwick, Kevin Day, Lillian Yee, and Hitomi Oba. A highlight of their recent work includes the 2023 premiere of Stefan Cwik’s Transfigurations, an ambitious interdisciplinary piece for brass quintet and orchestra that incorporates animated artwork.
