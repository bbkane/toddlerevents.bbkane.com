11:00 - 11:30 [Lunar New Year: Lion Dance with Buu Kim Tu Dragon and Lion Dance Association](https://sccl.bibliocommons.com/events/696ac9774a9fd98b7f781307)

A dynamic and visually striking tradition, the lion dance is a celebrated performance that symbolizes strength, courage, and prosperity. Skilled performers bring an intricately crafted lion to life through expressive, coordinated movements, all set to the pulse of rhythmic drums, cymbals, and gongs.
Often featured at festivals, community gatherings, and milestone events, the lion dance is especially meaningful during celebrations of the Lunar New Year. Its energetic movements and vibrant presence are believed to usher in good fortune and sweep away negative energy, making it a powerful way to welcome a new year with optimism and vitality.
