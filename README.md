A dynamic and visually striking tradition, the lion dance is a celebrated performance that symbolizes strength, courage, and prosperity. Skilled performers bring an intricately crafted lion to life through expressive, coordinated movements, all set to the pulse of rhythmic drums, cymbals, and gongs.

Its energetic movements and vibrant presence are believed to usher in good fortune and sweep away negative energy, making it a powerful way to welcome a new year with optimism and vitality.
